1.

To get started, click create a page, below the green sign up button.

2.

What kind of business do you have? Select a page type.

3.

Select a category for your business.

4.

Enter additional business information.

5.

If you don’t already have a Facebook account, follow the steps to create one. If you do, click “I already have a Facebook account”

6.

Log in to Facebook with your email addresss and password.

7.

Add a brief description of your company. Add your website, or another web address like Yelp or Twitter. It’s also okay if you leave this blank.

8.

Upload a profile picture. Your company logo will look great! You can upload one from your computer or import from a website.

9.

You can enable ads for your company. We recommend coming back to this later. Click skip.

10.

Your page is live! Let’s continue to get it set up. Be the first to like your page. Click the like button.

11.

There are still a few more basics to add. You’ll want to attract your current customers and new ones. Facebook has a built in functionality to invite people via email. Test it out by inviting yourself via your email address and howtocity@codeforamerica.org

12.

Share your first post. You can share text, photos, or a link.

13.

It’s important to connect to customers and other local business owners. Facebook is a great tool for this. Start by searching for How to City at the top of the page.

14.

Click the like button to like How to City.

15.

Write a comment, ask a question, or respond to one of the polls on the How to City page. We’ll promote your business’ page in return.

16.

To get back to your page, click the facebook logo at the top of the page. This takes you back to your newsfeed. In order to get to the insights page, click Insights in the left menu.

17.

CONGRATS! You now have a live Facebook page that’s ready for customers. In future lessons, we’ll show you how to use all of the features.
